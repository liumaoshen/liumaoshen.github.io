About me

I'm Eric Bouchut.
I write software and I'm really fond of dance and Ruby on Rails.
My focus is on Web Development.

I also have a solid hands-on experience in Java and a real knack for the art of teaching.

I’m a software developer with over 15 years of experience:

  • 6 years as a Web developer (Ruby on Rails, Java, GWT) for Antidot, a search engine company and LD Mobile editor of netADge a mobile DSP in the Real Time Bid arena (programmatic advertising).
  • 5 years of Java and Web development for 2 startups: Perform (acquired by BMC) and Miyowa
  • extensive work with Java including 7 years for the fortune 500 company BMC Software
  • 3 years of Unix/C/C++ development for an IT consulting firm
  • Computer science teacher for Axis Digital.
    • Taught 4 different training classes to groups of 15 people for 3 years.
    • Developped and gave the computer science class “Utilisation Unix avancée”

More recently I have joined the team at netADge as a Ruby on Rails and Java Developer.
